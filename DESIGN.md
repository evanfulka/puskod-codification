# Puskod NSN Portal - Design System (Googlestitch Pattern)

## 1. Color Palette
- **Primary (Maroon):** `#800000` (Identity: Puskod Kemhan Red)
- **Secondary (Gold):** `#D4AF37` (Identity: Excellence & Defense)
- **Background (Cream/Off-White):** `#FDFBF7` (As seen in NCAGE portal image)
- **Footer/Text Dark:** `#1A1A1A`
- **Surface:** `#FFFFFF`

## 2. Typography
- **Heading:** `Inter` or `Geist Sans` (Bold, Tracking: -0.02em)
- **Body:** `Inter` (Regular, Line-height: 1.6)

## 3. UI Components Rules
- **Buttons:** Rounded (8px), Padding: 12px 24px.
- **Cards:** White background, Soft shadow (shadow-sm), Border: 1px #E5E7EB.
- **Navbar:** Sticky top, Transparent with blur effect (glassmorphism) or Solid Off-White.

## 4. Brand Tone
- Professional, Transparent, Secure, and Official.
